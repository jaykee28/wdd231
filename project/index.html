Welcome to Rykaart’s Restaurant at Longridge Wine Estate

Nestled in the Cape Winelands at Longridge Wine Estate, Rykaart’s Restaurant offers seasonal family-style dining, locally inspired menus, and sweeping vineyard and mountain views in a relaxed countryside setting.

THE RYKAART’S

Rykaart’s at Longridge Wine Estate is a family-owned restaurant run by Alex and Ella Rykaart. They offer a three-course, family-style dining experience inspired by fresh, biodynamic produce from the estate. With years of experience in top Cape Winelands restaurants, Alex and Ella opened their first restaurant in Stellenbosch in 2021, earning a star at the Eat Out Restaurant Awards in their debut year. When the opportunity arose to grow and introduce a new concept, the Rykaart family—including baby Lara—relocated to Longridge Wine Estate. "We are excited to welcome our regular diners and new guests to our home at Longridge. Serving family-style meals paired with beautiful wines, we are proud to offer this very special culinary experience," say Alex and Ella Rykaart.

OUR CONCEPT

Rykaart’s at Longridge Wine Estate offers a wholesome three-course, family-style dining experience inspired by fresh produce from the biodynamic farm. Join us for a relaxed feast that feels like a home-cooked meal shared around the family table — informal, fun, and filled with a variety of delicious dishes to enjoy together.

OUR FOOD

At Rykaart’s we prepare a set three course menu served ‘family-style’ for the table to share. Our menu changes seasonally and is prepared using sustainable ingredients from Longridge Wine Estate and the surrounding farms. Most dietary requirements can be accommodated for if arranged with us when booking.
